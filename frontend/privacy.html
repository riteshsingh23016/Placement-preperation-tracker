Privacy Policy

Learn how Placement Prep Tracker collects, secures, and handles your professional data.

Last Updated: May 31, 2026

Welcome to Placement Prep Tracker. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explain how we collect, store, process, and protect your information when you register, coordinate, and prepare for career opportunities on our platform.

1. Account Registration

To access the platform, users must register an account. During registration, we collect:

  • Full Name: To identify you within institution portals and admin dashboards.
  • Email Address: Used as your unique account identifier, for sending verification links, and resetting passwords.
  • Hashed Password: We securely hash your password using industry-standard bcrypt hashing on our server. We never store your password in plaintext.

2. Email Verification & Security

To ensure validity and institution affiliation, new student accounts start in an unverified state. The system generates a secure, timed verification token sent to your email. Accounts remain locked until email verification is complete, protecting the platform from unauthorized access.

3. Password Reset & OTP Handling

If you request a password reset, the system generates a secure, numeric 6-digit One-Time Password (OTP) with a strict 15-minute expiration window. OTP requests are subject to server-side rate limits (maximum of 3 requests per 15 minutes) to protect against brute-force and spam abuse.

4. Student Profile Data

Students can voluntarily build and share details on their profile page to organize their placement readiness. This information includes:

  • Academic Credentials: College Name, course, branch, and graduation year.
  • Contact Info: Phone number and biography.
  • Professional Resumes & Links: LinkedIn profile links, GitHub links, and links to hosted resume documents.

5. Application & Placement Pipelines

The core feature of the platform is tracking career pipelines. We host the following data linked to your account:

  • Company name, job role, package details (LPA), status (e.g. Applied, Interviewing, Selected, Rejected), priority, application date, and custom interview notes.
  • This data is private to you and the platform coordinators, assisting you in visualizing progress.

6. Notes & Preparatory Data

Students can create, pin, and categorize preparation notes (e.g. DSA, System Design, DBMS cheatsheets) within customized collections. These notes are stored securely and remain private to the user's workspace.

7. Administrator Access & Moderation

Institution coordinators or system administrators are granted administrative accounts to oversee placement cycles. Administrators have permissions to:

  • Create and manage institutional placement drives (companies, packages, eligibility, deadlines).
  • Broadcast platform-wide system announcements.
  • Perform account moderation, including blocking/unblocking accounts for security compliance.

8. Email Notifications

The platform dispatches transactional emails essential to security and account operations. These include signup verification emails, OTP codes, and password modification alerts. We do not sell or share your email address for advertising purposes.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your profile, applications, and preparation notes as long as your account remains active. Users can update their profile or request deletion of their records at any time. When an account is removed, all linked notes, applications, and profile documents are scrubbed from the active database.

10. Cookies & Local Storage

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. We utilize standard Web Local Storage (`localStorage`) solely to maintain your authentication token state and preferred user interface theme (light or dark mode) for a smooth session experience.
